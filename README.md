# 🌟 Dream Guiders - Career Guidance Platform

Dream Guiders is a modern web-based career guidance platform designed to help students choose the right career path, explore opportunities, and plan their future effectively.

---

## 🚀 Features

* 🔐 **Authentication System**

  * Login & Signup functionality
* 🎨 **Multiple Themes**

  * Dark, Light, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Rose themes
* 🤖 **AI Chatbot (DreamBot)**

  * Helps users with career-related queries
* 📚 **Career Guidance**

  * Stream selection after 10th
  * Career options after 12th
* 🏫 **College Suggestions**

  * Explore colleges based on interest
* 📝 **Resume Builder**
* 📅 **Study Planner**
* 🔖 **Bookmarks System**
* ❓ **FAQ Section**

---
